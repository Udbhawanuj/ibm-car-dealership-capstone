About Us

We make dealership decisions clearer by combining transparent branch information, authentic customer reviews, and a team that believes buying a car should feel confident—not confusing.

A national network with a human point of view.

DriveSphere was created to help customers compare dealership branches across the United States. Our platform highlights location details, service experiences, verified purchases, and review sentiment in one responsive application.

The project uses Django, React, REST-style APIs, Docker, Kubernetes, and continuous integration to deliver a dependable full-stack experience.
